About the Reporter

Jaylyn Preslicka is a reporter pursuing her master's degree at Northwestern University, Medill School of Journalism.

In Los Angeles, Chicago and Washington, D.C., she will cover policy, mental health resources, government agencies and local/federal policies that directly impact margianlized communities.

With undergraduate degrees in journalism and sociology, Jaylyn uses data, qualitative and quantative interviews to highlight injustice in the public sector.
